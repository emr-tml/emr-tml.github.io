DroidRevive - Revive Old Android Devices Easily

How to?

1) Go to settings

2) Go to "About device"

3) Click 5-6 times fastly to "Build number" and it will say "You are a developer"

4) Find "Developer Settings" on settings

5) Find "Background process limit"

6) If your device is VERY slow, make it 2, if it's normal but a little slow, you can make it 3 or 4 :)

ENJOY!
